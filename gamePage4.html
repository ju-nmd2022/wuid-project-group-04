Make No Sound

Make no Sound is a distorted place that exists somewhere between dreams and reality, where children are left to face the horrors of the world alone, searching for hope.

Immerse yourself in Make No Sound, a dark whimsical tale that will confront you with your childhood fears as you help Six escape The Maw – a vast, mysterious vessel inhabited by corrupted souls looking for their next meal!
