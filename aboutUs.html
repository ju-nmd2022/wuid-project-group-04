About us

The purpose of our website is for small games/indie developers to be able to have their own platform and community without the many expenses and work that follow. It is an opportunity for them to get their dream in motion and promote their games. Meanwhile they get the chance to connect with other “small game”-owners from all over the world. Not only that, but our community strives to be inclusive of everyone interested in games, therefore it is also an open community for the players to interact with their favorite developers on our site, and each other.
