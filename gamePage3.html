Hypnosis

XBOX / PC / PS

Adventure / Strategy

Horrors wait inside S:t John's Sanctuary. A long-abandoned home for the mentally ill, that have been the attraction for many venturesome teenagers throughout the years. Sven Qvist, a 17 year old high schooler who gets dared to spend the night inside the Asylum alone.

Will he make it out in time before he loses his mind? Hypnosis is a true survival horror experience which aims to show that the most terrifying monsters of all come from the human mind.
