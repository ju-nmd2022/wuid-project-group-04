Runnig Fools

XBOX / PC / PS

Co-Op / Adventure / Puzzle

Running Fools is a hilarious, light-hearted game to enjoy with friends. It combines competition through a battle-royale style, and puzzles that you have to solve together in order to get to the next level.

You play as jellybean-like creatures with flexible psysics built to look silly as you make your way through the hundreds of levels with different mysteries and secrets.

Developers: Development Sweden
